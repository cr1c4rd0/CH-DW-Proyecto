Custom Murals is the creation of Mural Artist, Carolina Escobar I qualified with a Degree in Product Design Engineering. From there I went on to work within the Design industry, for both national and international markets.

Carolina's passion for design started at an early age. As a child, she was always in a constant search to renovate her own room. With the help of her parents, she continually sought to modify her personal space, trying new colours, types of furniture layout and creating her own decoration with crafts she learned from her mother and grandmother.

Carolina has found through her travels and the opportunity to have lived in several countries such as Colombia, Costa Rica, The United States and Canada, inspiration to incorporate in her designs unexpected combinations that seek to surprise and captivate hearts.

She is an award-winning and scholarship-winning graduated from Interior Decorating program at Sheridan College, recognized for her creative, conceptual, and analytical talents.

As a product design engineer, she finds in a successful design a perfect balance between functionality and aesthetics with a close relationship with the lifestyle.

Many years later, Expecting my son, I was looking at returning to work but was undecided as to what to do. I happened to be decorating my son's nursery room when I decided to paint by myself. It was this mural that gave me the inspiration to take the plunge and start the business.
